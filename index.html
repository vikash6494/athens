Athens

Overview

Athens, the capital city of Greece, is a major urban area in the Mediterranean and a key cultural and economic center in southeastern Europe. With a population of over 3.6 million in its urban area, it is the eighth-largest urban area in the European Union (EU). The Municipality of Athens has a population of 643,452 (2021) within its official limits, and covers an area of 38.96 km2 (15.04 sq mi).
